# Colosseum Main Submission Copy

AXIOM is a private AI credit and yield protocol on Solana.

Problem: DeFi credit is stuck between overcollateralized lending and centralized undercollateralized lending. Aave-style markets require excessive collateral. Maple-style markets rely on KYC, whitelists, and trust.

Solution: AXIOM lets borrowers prove creditworthiness privately. A QVAC agent scores cross-chain on-chain history locally. A ZK proof verifies the resulting credit tier on-chain. Borrowers can access USDT loans with lower collateral and no KYC.

For lenders, AXIOM routes idle USDT into Kamino when utilization is low and keeps liquidity available when borrower demand rises. The yield agent operates locally and every devnet action is bounded by Privy wallet policy plus AXIOM on-chain policy.

Why now: Solana provides cheap, fast settlement; USDT provides stable liquidity; QVAC makes local AI practical; Privy and AXIOM policy make AI-managed money safe on devnet; GoldRush, Birdeye, QuickNode, Kamino, and Torque provide the data, pricing, execution, yield, and growth rails. Ika remains the future decentralized MPC path when public Solana CPI is available.

AXIOM is not just another lending UI. It is a credit layer for Solana DeFi: private borrower reputation, policy-safe agent execution, and autonomous lender yield in one protocol.
